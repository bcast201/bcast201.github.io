My Developer Story

During some of the darkest days of the Pandemic in spring of 2020 I finally decided to pursue a longtime interest in web development by enrolling in an online coding bootcamp. Like so many others during that time I felt I was ready for a change so I moved on from my first career as a television newscast director and dove into a new challenge of creating beautiful, interactive webpages. A year later I was fortunate to receive my first job offer from Lifeboat Creative and quickly got to work learning the ropes of professional web development.

Since joining the Lifeboat team I've further honed my skills in CSS, HTML, and Javascript, along with gaining experience in PHP, Laravel, Wordpress and Expression Engine theme development, version control, and a lot of debugging!

If you'd like to know more about my development skills, work history, or the Nebraska Cornhuskers, feel free to reach out on LinkedIn!
